Lasagna

Lasagna is an italian cuisine dish made of lasagna sheets, tomato sauce, and mince meat. Easy to prepare, and incredibly tasty, this popular dish is a favourite for cosy weekend nights. Here, we will show you how to whip up an amazing dish in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • 400g lamb
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 celery stick
  • 1 red chilli
  • 2 tbsp. butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt and pepper
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp. tomato paste
  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 160g cheddar
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp. fresh rosemary
  • creme friache

Directions

  1. Peel and dice the carrot, onion and celery. Cut and chop the chilli and peel and thinly slice the garlic.
  2. Fry the minced lamb on a low heat in butter for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the diced vegetables, tomatoes, tomato paste, rosemary and bayleaves.
  4. Heat over a low heat for at least 20 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Heat the oven to 200 °.
  6. Place 1/3 of the crème fraîche in the bottom in an ovenproof dish.
  7. Then layer the lasagne sheets, minced meat sauce and cheese into the dish. Finish with crème fraîche and cheese.
  8. Cook in the middle of the oven for about 30 minutes.
