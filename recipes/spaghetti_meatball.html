Spaghetti and Meatballs

Spaghetti and meatballs is an italian cuisine dish made of spaghetti, tomato sauce, and meatballs (mostly lamb). Easy to prepare, and incredibly tasty, this popular dish is a favourite for cosy weekend nights. Here, we will show you how to whip up an amazing dish in less than an hour.

Ingredients

  • 450g spaghetti
  • 450g minced beef
  • 30g bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp. chopped parsley
  • 25g parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • salt
  • 1/2 tsp. chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 40g onion, chopped
  • 2 x 400g chopped tomato tins
  • 1 bay leaf
  • freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook spaghetti according to is pack instructions. Drain after.
  2. In a large bowl, combine beef with bread crumbs, parsley, parmesan cheese, egg, garlic, salt, and chilli flakes. Mix well, then form 16 balls.
  3. In a large pot over medium heat, pour oil. Add the meatballs, then cook, turning them occasionally. Once browned, move them to a plate.
  4. Add onions to a pot and cook well. Add crushed tomatoes and bay leaf, then season with salt and pepper. Bring to a simmer, then return the meatballs to the pot and cover. Simmer until sauce has thickened, around 10 minutes.
  5. The pasta is done. Serve it accordingly, and top with some extra parmesan cheese.
